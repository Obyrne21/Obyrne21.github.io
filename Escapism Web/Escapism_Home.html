Escapism

Escapism is a term that describes modern America. People of all ages seek to escape from the harsh worlds we've come to know. They try to leave their world of trouble, struggle and stress behind because it takes a toll on the mind. This website is dedicated to studying ways students and other citizens escape the real word. We've specifically focused on literature, cinema, and virtual games but our studies and experiences aren't just limited to the three.

Definition

es·cap·ism

/əˈskāpˌizəm/

noun

the tendency to seek distraction and relief from unpleasant realities, especially by seeking entertainment or engaging in fantasy.

synonyms: fantasy, fantasizing, daydreaming, daydreams, reverie
